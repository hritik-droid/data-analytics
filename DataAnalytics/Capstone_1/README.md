# Capstone 1: Business Analysis with SQL and Excel

## Territory

Massachusetts (Sales Manager: Bo Heap)
Region: Northeast

---

## Project Overview

This project analyzes sales performance for the Massachusetts territory using SQL and Excel. The goal is to answer key business questions related to revenue, trends, product performance, and store performance, and to provide a recommendation for where to focus sales efforts in the next quarter.

---

## Tools Used

* MySQL Workbench (SQL queries and data analysis)
* Microsoft Excel (PivotTable and PivotChart)
* GitHub (project repository)

---

## Analysis Performed

### 1. Total Revenue and Date Range

Calculated total revenue for the Massachusetts territory and identified the start and end dates of the dataset to understand the overall performance and time coverage.

### 2. Monthly Revenue Breakdown

Analyzed revenue by month to identify trends and fluctuations in sales over time.

### 3. Territory vs Region Comparison

Compared Massachusetts territory revenue with the Northeast region to evaluate relative performance.

### 4. Transactions and Average Transaction Size by Category

Examined the number of transactions and average transaction size by product category to understand customer behavior and category performance.

### 5. Store Performance Ranking

Ranked stores within the Massachusetts territory based on total revenue to identify top-performing and lower-performing locations.

---

## Key Findings

* Revenue varies across months, indicating seasonal or demand-based fluctuations.
* Certain product categories generate higher revenue and transaction activity.
* Some stores significantly outperform others within the territory.

---

## Recommendation

Based on the analysis, sales efforts next quarter should focus on:

* High-performing product categories that generate the most revenue
* Strong-performing months to maximize sales opportunities
* Improving lower-performing stores through targeted promotions or operational adjustments

This approach can help increase overall sales performance in the Massachusetts territory.
